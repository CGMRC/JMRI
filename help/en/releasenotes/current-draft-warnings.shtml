• The way defaults for Digitrax PR2, PR3 and PR4 connections are handled has changed. Existing PR2, PR3 and PR4 connections might give a "The Defaults preferences are invalid" warning when saving preferences. In that case, click "yes" and then go set all the default radio buttons on the "Defaults" pane to the LocoNet connection. (You can also click "no", but you'll get the message next time you store too)
